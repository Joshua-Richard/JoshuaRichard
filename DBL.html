    • A Redesgin of the existing Demerara Bank Limited website users interface.

    This is a project that i created myself to test my self and to showcase my skils and understanding of designing.

    Problem

    I wanted to create a design that was more modern and attractive but i did not want to change the contents and colors. One of the biggest issues i had were with the colors that were used in the original website are a shade of green and gold.

    solution

    To combat the issue of the colors that would be used i gave myslef a bit of an advantage by allowing myself in using other shades of green and gold but at the same time remaining close to the original colors.

    Tools

    • Adobe XD
    • Canva
    • AAA Logo
    • Photoshop

    My Role

    • UI design

    Timeline

    • Overall: 3 weeks
    • Design & testing: 2 weeks

    Wireframes

    Using Adobe XD, I created a few low-fidelity wireframes. In which i added the infromation found on the official Demerara Bank website. At this stage, the wireframes were defined enough for some user testing. Based on multiple tests, i've made a few alternations and moved on to creating high-fidelity prototypes.

    UI Design

    Once the usiability issues were resolved, i moved on to design the final screens in Adobe XD. My goal was to create a visual identity that's aligned with the brand's values and message, which is: "Come grow with us". i've also checked other banking websites and took a deep dive into my catalog of references for inspiration.

    • The visual style i was aimimg for was that of a corporate and modern look, Because of the website being one where clients and potentional clients would visit on a regular basis.
    • The platform that i designed for were Laptops and Desktops
